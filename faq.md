# Questions, answered

      
If you don't see what you're looking for, reach out directly &mdash; I'm happy to talk it through.

    
  

  
    
      

        
          What is life coaching, exactly?
          
Life coaching is a structured, ongoing conversation focused on where you are now and where you want to go. It's forward-looking and action-oriented &mdash; less about analyzing the past, more about building your next steps.

        

        
          How is this different from therapy?
          
Therapy often focuses on healing and mental health. Coaching focuses on growth, direction, and action for people who are generally doing well but want more clarity, accountability, and momentum. If I think therapy would serve you better, I'll say so.

        

        
          How long are sessions?
          
Most sessions run 60 minutes. 

        

        
          Do you offer packages?
