# Privacy Policy

      
Last updated: July 2026

    
  

  
    
      
Joyfully Present Life Coaching LLC ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

      
## 1. Information We Collect

      

        - **Contact details** you give us when booking: name, email, and phone number.

        - **Account information** if you sign in with Google or create an account with us — your name and email.

        - **Booking and session details**: appointment times, the service or package booked, and session status.

        - **Payment information**: when you pay by card or PayPal, your payment details are handled directly by Stripe or PayPal — we never see or store your full card number. For Zelle/Venmo we only see what you choose to send us directly through those apps.

      

      
## 2. How We Use Your Information

      
We use your information to schedule and manage appointments, send booking confirmations and invoices, provide your session link, respond to questions, and maintain our business records as required by law.

      
## 3. Cookies & Sessions

      
We use a small number of essential cookies to keep you signed in — for example, after signing in with Google or our own account system, or when you're logged into the admin portal. We don't use advertising or tracking cookies.

      
## 4. Third-Party Service Providers

      
We share information with trusted service providers only as needed to run our business:

      

        - **Stripe** and **PayPal** — payment processing

        - **Google** — optional sign-in

        - **Resend** — sending booking confirmations and invoice emails

        - **Cloudflare** — website hosting and infrastructure

        - **BetterStack** — uptime monitoring for our systems

      

      
These providers only receive what they need to perform their function and are not permitted to use your information for their own purposes.

      
## 5. Data Retention

      
We keep booking and payment records for as long as needed to run our business and meet legal/tax obligations, and delete or anonymize information we no longer need.

      
## 6. Your Rights

      
You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time by emailing [hello@joyfullypresent.life](mailto:hello@joyfullypresent.life). If you're a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA); contact us to make a request.

      
## 7. Children's Privacy

      
Our services are intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

      
## 8. Security

      
We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and access controls on our systems. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data appropriately for a business of our size.

      
## 9. Changes to This Policy

      
We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent changes.

      
## 10. Contact Us

      
Questions about this policy? Reach us at [hello@joyfullypresent.life](mailto:hello@joyfullypresent.life) or 440 N Barranca Ave #9885, Covina, CA 91723.
